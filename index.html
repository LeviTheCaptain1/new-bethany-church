About Our Church

We are a close-knit Nepali community church dedicated to sharing the gospel, preserved in our rich cultural heritage. Whether you are new to the area or searching for a spiritual home, we offer a safe haven of true Christian fellowship, mutual care, and hope.

Our mission is simple: to exalt God, nurture disciples across generations, and extend compassionate help to our families both locally and back home.
